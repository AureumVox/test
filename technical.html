Microsoft Excel 2010/2013 - Pivot Tables

PivotTables is one of the most powerful reporting features that Excel has to offer, and yet is also one of the least understood and most underutilised functions available. In this 3.5 hour crash course on PivotTables you will learn not only how to create pivot tables, but how to manipulate the data, summarise the data, and present the data in the exact format that works best for your reporting needs.

Once you’ve finished this course you will be the office Guru on PivotTables.

Topics Covered - 3.5 Hours

  1. Introduction to PivotTables
  2. Creating and Pivoting PivotTables
  3. Summarising PivotTable Data
  4. Sorting and Filtering PivotTable Data
  5. Formatting PivotTables
  6. Applying Conditional Formatting to PivotTables
  7. Creating and Manipulating PivotCharts
